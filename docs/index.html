Stacks

Stacks is a fresh twist on classic poker and puzzle games! Arrange cards on the board to create the strongest hands horizontally, vertically, in the corners, and even in the discard stack. Every hand you build boosts your score, and better poker hands earn higher multipliers. Whether you’re a casual player or a poker pro, Stacks offers endless replayability with: Dynamic scoring: Every direction counts—maximize your points with clever placement! Multiple modes: Enjoy light and dark themes to suit your style and mood. Achievements: Unlock trophies as you master new strategies and reach higher scores. Intuitive design: Easy to pick up, tough to master, and perfect for quick games or deep sessions. How high can you stack your score? Download Stacks and find out!
